  • Careers at Southern Reach
    We're looking for top talent in a diverse set of fields including biology, anthropology, land surveying, linguistics, and psychology. We're looking for people who want to work on the cutting edge, who want to be hands-on and work out in the field.
    Why Work for Us?

    As a researcher, your expedition will include one month of training before being deployment into the field. After training, you'll go on a two-week expedition into a hotbed of unexplored wilderness where you'll conduct specialized research.

Welcome to Southern Reach

With over one hundred scientific breakthroughs in the last year alone, Southern Reach has consistently proven it is on the cutting edge of research. Come find out why Southern Reach is an innovator in science and research.

Our Methods

Why expeditions?

At Southern Reach, we believe that scientific breakthroughs rarely happen in the lab. Instead, we find that they happen in the field. We've found one of the most active areas for scientific discoveries and we're sending in top talent to explore it.

More about our expeditions
Life sciences

Southern Reach is an undisputed leader in a variety of biological fields from mycology to entomology. And we're just getting started.

Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics is one of the top fields of study at Southern Reach due to the extensive amount of data we collect from expeditions.

Organic chemistry

Southern Research has twice revolutionized the field of organic chemistry through discoveries made on scientific expeditions.

Medical research

At Southern Reach, we've dealt with a variety of human diseases—some of which are completely new to the medical community.
